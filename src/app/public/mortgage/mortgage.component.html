Common Questions

Everything you need to know about home financing.

What is the minimum down payment?

Many first-time buyer programs allow down payments as low as 3.5% or even 3% for conventional loans.

How does credit score affect my rate?

Generally, a higher credit score qualifies you for lower interest rates, which can save you thousands over the life of the loan.

What is Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI)?

PMI is usually required if your down payment is less than 20%. It protects the lender but helps you buy a home sooner.

Should I lock my interest rate?

Locking your rate protects you from market increases during the loan processing period, which typically takes 30-45 days.
